## ⚡ Quick Installation (15 minutes)

### For Complete Beginners - Follow These 4 Steps:

1. **Extract the ZIP file**
   - Right-click → Extract All

2. **Create FREE Cloudflare account**
   - Go to cloudflare.com
   - Sign up (free forever)

3. **Upload your website**
   - Click "Workers & Pages" → "Upload assets"
   - Drag all HTML, CSS, JS files
   - Click "Deploy"

4. **Connect your domain**
   - Click "Custom domains"
   - Add: bcesoberliving.com
   - Update nameservers at your domain registrar

**DONE! Your site is live!** 🚀

## 🆘 Common Issues & Solutions

### "Website not loading"
**Solution**: Wait 15-30 minutes after deployment, clear browser cache

### "Domain not connecting"
**Solution**: Verify nameservers at domain registrar, wait up to 24 hours

### "Form not working"
**Solution**: Check that Worker is deployed (see README.md section on Workers)

### "Images not showing"
**Solution**: Verify image files uploaded, check file names match HTML

### "Mobile menu not opening"
**Solution**: Make sure script.js is uploaded and loaded
