DESIGN ELEMENTS:
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

🎨 GRAFFITI TEXT: "BCE"
   • Impact font (bold, street-art style)
   • Faded blue color (#4da6ff)
   • Stroke outline for depth
   • Slightly roughed edges

Ⅲ  ROMAN NUMERALS: "III"
   • Below BCE text
   • Lighter blue (#6bb8ff)
   • Honors William's brother
   • Smaller, subtle font

💙 HEART OUTLINE
   • Hand-drawn style
   • Surrounds entire text
   • Faded blue stroke
   • Symbolizes love & compassion

💧 PAINT DRIPS
   • Two small drips below text
   • Authentic spray-paint effect
   • Adds graffiti credibility
   • Subtle opacity (30-40%)

✨ ANIMATION
   • Gentle rotation & scale
   • Glowing effect pulses
   • 4-second smooth loop
   • Professional but dynamic
