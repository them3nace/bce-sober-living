A Message from Our Founder

"I vow to help and provide a safe, secure, and structured environment for the citizens of this town. I will do everything in my power to save the ones that want it. The people who want what I and many others have assiduously achieved: Peace, happiness, and love."

— William Edwards, Founder

BCE Sober Living was born from personal tragedy and transformed into a mission of hope. Named after William's brother who passed from an accidental overdose, BCE represents both remembrance and redemption—a commitment to help those who are truly ready to stop and build a life worth living.
