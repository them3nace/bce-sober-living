## 🚀 Deploy in 3 Easy Steps

### Step 1: Extract Files
- Download bce_fullsite.zip
- Extract all files to a folder

### Step 2: Create Cloudflare Account
- Go to cloudflare.com
- Sign up (FREE forever)
- Add your domain: bcesoberliving.com

### Step 3: Upload & Launch
- Go to Workers & Pages
- Click "Upload assets"
- Drag all HTML, CSS, JS files
- Click "Deploy"
- Connect your domain
- DONE! 🎉
